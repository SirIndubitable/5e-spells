Vitriolic Sphere
4th-level Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 150 feet
Components: V, S, M (Giant slug bile)
Duration: Instantaneous
Each creature in a 20-foot radius centered on a point you choose must make a Dexterity save. On failure, it takes 10d4 acid damage and 5d4 acid damage at the end of its next turn. On a success, it takes half the initial damage and no extra damage.
At Higher Levels: The initial damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 4th.
