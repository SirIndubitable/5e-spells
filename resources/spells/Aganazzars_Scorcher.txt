Aganazzar's Scorcher
2nd-level Evocation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S, M (Red dragon's scale)
Duration: Instantaneous
A line of fire 30 feet long and 5 feet wide shoots from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity save. A creature takes 3d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half damage on a success.
At Higher Levels: The damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
