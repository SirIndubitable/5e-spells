Bane
1st-level enchantment
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V, S, M (Drop of blood)
Duration: Up to 1 minute
Up to three visible creatures of your choice must make Charisma saving throws. If a target fails, whenever they make an attack roll or saving throw before the spell ends, they must roll a d4 and subtract the number rolled.
At Higher Levels: You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
