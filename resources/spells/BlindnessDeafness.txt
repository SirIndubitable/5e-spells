Blindness/Deafness
2nd-level Necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 30 feet
Components: V
Duration: 1 minute
Choose a creature that you can see to make a Constitution save. If it fails, the target is either your choice of blinded or deafened. At the end of its turns, the target can repeat the save. On a success, the spell ends.
At Higher Levels: You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
