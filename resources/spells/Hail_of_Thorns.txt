Hail of Thorns
1st-level conjuration
Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Range: Self
Components: V
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
The next time you hit with a ranged weapon attack, in addition to the normal effects of the attack, the target and each creature within 5 feet of it must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 1d10 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
At Higher Levels: The damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st (to a maximum of 6d10).
