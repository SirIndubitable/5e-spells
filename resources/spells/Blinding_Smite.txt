Blinding Smite
3rd-level Evocation
Casting Time: 1 bonus action
Range: Self
Components: V
Duration: Concentration, Up to 1 minute
The next creature you hit with a melee weapon attack must pass a Constitution save or be blinded until the spell ends. Your attack also deals an extra 3d8 radiant damage. The blinded creature makes a save at the end of its turns, and is no longer blinded on a success.
