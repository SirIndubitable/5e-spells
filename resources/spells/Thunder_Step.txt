Thunder Step
3rd-level Conjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 90 feet
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous
You teleport yourself to an empty space you can see. Each creature within 10 feet of the space you left must make a Constitution save, taking 3d10 thunder damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. Thunder booms out and can be heard from up to 300 feet away.
You can bring along objects as long as you don't exceed your carrying capacity, and a willing creature of your size or smaller who isn't exceeding its carrying capacity.Â It must be within 5 feet of you when you cast this spell and there must be an available space within 5 feet of your destination. Otherwise, the creature is left behind.
At Higher Levels: The damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 3rd.
