Toll the Dead
Necromancy cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous
The target must pass a Wisdom save or take damage. If it's missing any hit points, the damage is 1d12 necrotic damage; otherwise, it's 1d8.
The damage increases by one die when you reach 5th, 11th, and 17th level.
