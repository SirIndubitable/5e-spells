Calm Emotions
2nd-level Enchantment
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, Up to 1 minute
Each humanoid in a 20-foot-radius sphere must make a Charisma save. A creature can choose to fail this save. If a creature fails its saving throw, choose one of the following effects:
When the spell ends, the creature becomes hostile again, unless the DM rules otherwise.
