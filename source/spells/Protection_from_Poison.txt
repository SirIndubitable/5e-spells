Protection from Poison
2nd-level Abjuration
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 hour
You touch a creature. If it is poisoned, you neutralize the poison. If there's more than one poison, you neutralize one you know is present, or one at random. For the duration, the target has advantage on saving throws against being poisoned, and it has resistance to poison damage.
