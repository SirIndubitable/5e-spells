Commune with City
5th-level Divination
Casting Time: 1 minute (Ritual)
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous
Aboveground, this spell gives knowledge of the area within 1 mile of you. In sewers and other underground settings, you gain knowledge of the area within 600 feet of you.
