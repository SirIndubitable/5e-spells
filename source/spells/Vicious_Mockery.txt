Vicious Mockery
Enchantment cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous
Choose a target you can see. If it can hear you, it must pass a Wisdom save or take 1d4 psychic damage and have disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn.
The damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th, 11th, and 17th level.
